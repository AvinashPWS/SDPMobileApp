Volunteer Services

Most of the activities in the Sai Datta Peetham are carried out by the dedicated efforts of volunteers.

Nithya Annadanam is being planned in Sai Datta Peetham. Several Charity works are being planned where the volunteers in peetham will distribute food, clothes to the needy. Sai Bhajans and Satsangs are conducted every Thursday. As part of pallaki seva, Sai Pallaki will be taken to devotee's houses to bring peace and cure their ailments.

We need a lot of support from volunteers to carry out the above mentioned activities. We strongly urge you to sign up for any volunteer activity and get your family members and friends involved.
